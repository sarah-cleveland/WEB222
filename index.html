What makes X-Badge Products so special?


We back with flair.

Each badge is stitched directly onto a high-quality wool felt backing in the color of your choice. There are dozens of colors to choose from, and all of it is pre-shrunk, with the option of iron-on or pin backing.

We floss regularly.

All of our designs are stitched using premium colorfast DMC embroidery floss, which means your colors won't run in the wash. With hundreds of colors to choose from, you can customize your badge even further.

We stitch with love.

Every one of our badges and kits is hand-made, meaning there will be no other badge like it in the world. There are default packages to choose from if you don't want to bother with too much customization.
