About Us

We specialize in crossing sew-on, pin-on, and iron-on patches with cross-stitch patterns. These patches can easily be sewn onto clothing, bags, and other accessories.

Each badge is hand-stitched with embroidery thread onto a piece of wool felt in the colors of your choice, allowing the badges to be fully customizable. Patterns are available to begin, but if you have a special request, then we are able to negotiate that as well. DIY kits include felt, thread, waste cloth (for the cross-stitch grid), a patch backing to preserve the integrity of the strings, an embroidery needle, and a set of instructions on how to make your own patch.
